Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my porta potties?

We recommend booking at least 1–2 weeks in advance, especially during peak event seasons, but we can also handle last-minute and emergency rentals.

What’s included in the rental price?

Our rates include delivery, pickup, initial cleaning, and servicing based on the rental duration. Additional servicing can be arranged.

How often do you clean the units?

We offer weekly service as standard, but daily and custom cleaning schedules are available.

How many units do I need for my event?

As a general guideline, one unit per 75 guests for a 4-hour event. We’ll help you calculate the exact number based on headcount and duration.

Do your units meet ADA requirements?

Yes, we have ADA-compliant units available for all types of events and projects.

Can I get same-day delivery?

We offer same-day delivery for most rentals in Wilson, especially for emergencies or job site delays. Call early to confirm availability.
